How To Play?
  1. Enter number of tosses in one trial.
  2. Guess either heads will be more (or) tails will be more.
  3. Enter the bet on your choice within the limit.
  4. Click on Toss Button To Play.
  5. Go on Clicking until one gameplay is finished.
  6. Click PlayAgain To Play One More Time.
To Win The Game ? Get more than 100/- in Your Wallet within 10 Trials to complete the game for that gameplay.
Gaming Terms:-
  • Bet Lost= Total Bet Won By Computer ;
  • Bet Won = Total Bet Won By Player ;
  • Tosses = Number Of Coins To Toss At Once;
  • 1 Trial=Tossing 'n' Coins At Once.
  • 1 GamePlay=10 Trials
Game Scoring Rules:-
  1. If Your Guess Is Correct Then You Wallet Will Be Awarded With The Bet You Kept.
  2. If Your Guess Is InCorrect Then You Wallet Will Be Deducted With The Bet You Kept.
